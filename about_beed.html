About SMIT

The first ever technical school in Tacurong and in the whole province of Sultan Kudarat started as operation in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and eightyfive (1985). Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology or S.M.I.T as it is aptly called is originally located at the old Ventura building near Bautista Clinic in Tacurong, Sultan Kudarat. Later on, it transferred to the Sandoval building along Alunan Highway, Tacurong, Sultan Kudarat.
